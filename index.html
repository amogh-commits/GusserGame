Welcome to the Number Guesser Game!

The goal is to guess the correct number between 1 and 40.
The game will give you clues by coloring the balls based on your guess:

  • Yellow: Your guess is too low.
  • Red: Your guess is too high.
  • Green: You've guessed the correct number!

You have 6 attempts to find the correct number. Good luck!
